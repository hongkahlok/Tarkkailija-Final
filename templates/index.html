Tarkkailija

(Tark-kai-li-ja)

Observer in Finnish. With new challenges, comes the need for innovative solutions, and this is an answer to one of them.


Why?

What does this do?

Tarkkailija is meant to help tackle the issue of group gatherings, as they have a higher chance of a spreading viruses between humans. By reducing the size of potential group gatherings/density of people in an area, it directly reduces the probability of a virus spreading. Tarkkailija is being able to accurately count the amount of people going in and out of a building, and informs the user about the current amount occupants in an area.


How?

What makes it tick?

The core of Tarkkailija is OpenCV, a computer vision library that is able to extrapolate people data from a feed, and display it back to the user via the Flask web server, running on Python 3.9. Uses the MobileNetSSD model.
